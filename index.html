Tunnel Vision? It's all about Control

Tunnel Vision was my way getting back into coding for fun while providing solution to an extremely niche situation. I spend most of my time on social networks on Twitter and while I get meaningful content from certain followers, there are certain times I log on to check out only a couple of accounts and I find myself scrolling and clicking after hours. Tunnel Vision was the way of eliminating that ever occuring excuse.
